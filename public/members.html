How To Become A Member

Membership is open to any Pharmaceutical companies established in the Federation that fulfils the below listed conditions:

  • Has and utilises local manufacturing facilities for the production of drugs and medicines, from local and imported raw materials
  • is a member of manufacturers association of Nigeria(MAN).
  • is duly registered by national agency for food and drug administration control (NAFDAC) for drug manufacturing.
  • is seen to uphold the tenets of good manufacturing practice (GMP) which is the motto of the group. this is ascertained by the group's Quality Manager's committee before full ratification and such audit visits are repeated at a regular interval after full membership.
